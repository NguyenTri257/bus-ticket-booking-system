## Creating an Account

### Registration

1. Click **"Register"** or **"Sign Up"** in the top right corner
2. Fill in the registration form:
   - Email address
   - Password (minimum 8 characters)
   - Full name
   - Phone number (optional)
3. Click **"Create Account"**
4. Check your email for verification link
5. Click the verification link to activate your account

### Benefits of Registration

- Save your booking history
- Faster checkout process
- Manage multiple bookings
- Receive trip reminders
- Rate and review trips

### Login

1. Click **"Login"**
2. Enter your email and password
3. Click **"Sign In"**

**Forgot Password?**

1. Click **"Forgot Password"**
2. Enter your email address
3. Check your email for reset link
4. Click link and enter new password

### Google Sign-In

Alternatively, you can sign in with your Google account:

1. Click **"Sign in with Google"**
2. Select your Google account
3. Authorize the application
4. Complete your profile (if needed)

## Searching for Trips

### Basic Search

1. On the homepage, you'll see the search form
2. Fill in the search criteria:
   - **Origin**: Select departure city (e.g., "Ho Chi Minh City")
   - **Destination**: Select arrival city (e.g., "Hanoi")
   - **Date**: Choose travel date
   - **Passengers**: Number of passengers (default: 1)
3. Click **"Search"**

### Search Results

The results page displays:

- List of available trips
- Departure and arrival times
- Duration
- Price per seat
- Bus type (Standard, Limousine, Sleeper)
- Operator information
- Available seats
- Amenities (WiFi, AC, Toilet, Entertainment)

### Advanced Filters

Refine your search using filters on the left sidebar:

**Departure Time:**

- Morning (6:00 - 12:00)
- Afternoon (12:00 - 18:00)
- Evening (18:00 - 22:00)
- Night (22:00 - 6:00)

**Price Range:**

- Use the slider to set minimum and maximum price
- Or enter specific amounts

**Bus Type:**

- Standard: Regular seating
- Limousine: Premium seating with extra legroom
- Sleeper: Reclinable beds for overnight travel

**Amenities:**

- WiFi
- Air Conditioning
- Toilet
- Entertainment (TV/Music)

### Sorting Options

Sort results by:

- **Price**: Lowest to highest or highest to lowest
- **Time**: Earliest to latest departure
- **Duration**: Shortest to longest trip

## Making a Booking

### Passenger Information

After selecting seats, provide passenger details for each seat:

**Required Information:**

- Full Name (as on ID document)
- Phone Number (optional but recommended)
- ID/Passport Number (optional but may be required at boarding)

### Contact Information

Provide booking contact details:

- Email address (for e-ticket delivery)
- Phone number (for SMS updates)

**Note:** Guest users must provide contact information for booking lookup.

### Review Booking

Before payment, review:

- Trip details (date, time, route)
- Selected seats
- Passenger information
- Pricing breakdown:
  - Subtotal (seat prices)
  - Service fee (3% + fixed fee)
  - **Total Amount**

### Terms and Conditions

- Read cancellation policy
- Check luggage allowance
- Review boarding instructions
- Accept terms and conditions

Click **"Proceed to Payment"** when ready.

## Payment

### Payment Methods

Choose from:

1. **PayOS** - Credit/Debit cards, e-wallets
2. **Momo** - Mobile wallet
3. **ZaloPay** - Digital wallet
4. **Stripe** - International cards

### Payment Process

1. Select payment method
2. Enter payment details
3. Review amount
4. Confirm payment

### Payment Confirmation

After successful payment:

- Booking status changes to "Confirmed"
- E-ticket is generated automatically
- E-ticket sent to your email within minutes
- SMS confirmation (if phone provided)

### Failed Payment

If payment fails:

- Booking remains in "Pending" status
- Seats remain locked for 10 minutes
- You can retry payment
- After 10 minutes, booking is automatically cancelled

## Managing Bookings

### View Your Bookings

**For Registered Users:**

1. Login to your account
2. Click on your profile/name
3. Select **"My Bookings"**
4. View all your bookings

**Booking List Shows:**

- Booking reference number
- Trip details
- Status (Pending, Confirmed, Completed, Cancelled)
- Total price
- Actions (View, Cancel, Download ticket)

### Booking Details

Click on any booking to view:

- Complete trip information
- Passenger list
- Payment details
- E-ticket (PDF download)
- QR code for boarding

### Download E-Ticket

1. Open booking details
2. Click **"Download E-Ticket"** button
3. PDF file will be downloaded
4. Save or print for boarding

**E-Ticket Includes:**

- Booking reference
- QR code
- Trip details (date, time, route)
- Passenger information
- Seat numbers
- Boarding instructions

### Cancel Booking

1. Open booking details
2. Click **"Cancel Booking"** button
3. Select cancellation reason
4. Confirm cancellation
5. Refund will be processed according to cancellation policy

**Cancellation Policy:**

- Cancellations made 24+ hours before departure: 80% refund
- Cancellations made less than 24 hours: May incur penalties
- No-show: No refund

## FAQs

### General Questions

**Q: Do I need to create an account to book?**
A: No, guest checkout is available. However, registered users get additional benefits.

**Q: How do I receive my ticket?**
A: E-tickets are sent to your email immediately after payment confirmation.

**Q: Can I change my travel date?**
A: Currently, date changes are not supported. You must cancel and rebook (subject to cancellation policy).

**Q: What if I miss my bus?**
A: Missed trips are considered no-shows and are non-refundable.

### Payment Questions

**Q: Which payment methods are accepted?**
A: PayOS, Momo, ZaloPay, and Stripe (international cards).

**Q: Is payment secure?**
A: Yes, all payments are processed through secure, PCI-compliant gateways.

**Q: How long does refund take?**
A: Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days to original payment method.

### Booking Questions

**Q: How many seats can I book at once?**
A: You can book up to 10 seats per booking.

**Q: How long are seats locked for?**
A: Seats are locked for 10 minutes after selection. Complete payment within this time.

**Q: Can I select specific seats?**
A: Yes, use the interactive seat map to choose your preferred seats.

**Q: What if my preferred seat is taken?**
A: Choose an alternative seat or search for other trips.

### Trip Questions

**Q: What should I bring to board?**
A: Bring your e-ticket (printed or digital) and ID document.

**Q: Where do I board the bus?**
A: Boarding location is mentioned in your e-ticket. Arrive 15 minutes early.

**Q: What's the luggage allowance?**
A: Standard allowance is 20kg (2 pieces). Check trip details for specific policy.

**Q: Are meals provided?**
A: Depends on the trip duration and operator. Check trip amenities.

### Account Questions

**Q: I forgot my password. How do I reset it?**
A: Click "Forgot Password" on login page and follow email instructions.

**Q: How do I change my email?**
A: Login and go to Profile Settings to update email.

**Q: Can I delete my account?**
A: Contact customer support to request account deletion.
